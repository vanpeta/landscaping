About S & P Hanson Landscaping

S & P Hanson Landscaping is a family owned and operated business in the Central Valley since 1992. In 1976, Steve Hanson graduated with a degree in Ornamental Horticulture from California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo.

After years of experience in landscape design and construction management, Steve established S & P Hanson Landscaping in 1992.
